1 Abstract

This project presents the design and development of a Self-Optimizing Solar Electric Vehicle (Solar EV) integrated with an AI-Based Energy Management System (EMS). The system intelligently manages power flow between the solar panel, battery pack, and motor drive to maximize efficiency and extend vehicle range.


Using real-time sensor data such as battery State of Charge (SoC), solar input power, motor load, and driving conditions, the AI controller dynamically selects the optimal power source.

2 Introduction

With the rapid growth of electric vehicles and renewable energy systems, integrating solar energy into EVs provides a sustainable and eco-friendly solution. However, fluctuating solar availability and varying load demand create energy management challenges.


This project introduces an intelligent energy optimization system that uses AI-based logic to manage energy flow between solar panels, battery storage, and the motor system in real time.

3 Problem Statement

  • Solar energy availability is inconsistent
  • Inefficient power switching leads to energy loss
  • Limited battery capacity reduces driving range
  • Lack of intelligent prediction-based energy optimization in small-scale Solar EVs

4 Objectives

  • To design a compact Solar EV prototype
  • To implement AI-based real-time energy optimization
  • To monitor solar input, battery SoC, and motor load
  • To enable automatic switching between solar and battery power
  • To improve overall vehicle efficiency and range

5 System Components

  • Solar Panel
  • MPPT Charge Controller
  • Lithium-Ion Battery Pack
  • ESP32 / Arduino Microcontroller (AI Controller)
  • Current Sensor (ACS712)
  • Voltage Sensor Module
  • Motor Controller
  • BLDC Motor
  • Regenerative Braking System
  • LCD Display / IoT Dashboard

6 System Architecture

Solar Panel→MPPT→Battery Pack→Motor Controller→BLDC Motor
Sensors (V + I)→Microcontroller→Power Switching Logic
System Schematic Diagram

7 Working Principle

  • Solar panels generate electricity during sunlight
  • MPPT maximizes solar charging efficiency
  • Sensors continuously measure voltage, current, and load demand
  • The AI algorithm analyzes Battery SoC, Solar power input, Motor load demand, and Driving conditions
  • System automatically supplies power directly from solar, charges the battery, switches to battery mode, or activates regenerative braking
  • Data is displayed on LCD and uploaded to IoT cloud

8 AI-Based Energy Management Logic

Rule-Based AI Logic (Initial Stage)

IF Solar Power > Motor Demand
  THEN Use Solar Directly

IF Battery SoC < 20%
  THEN Activate Energy Saving Mode

IF Braking Detected
  THEN Enable Regenerative Charging

IF Solar Power Low AND Battery > 50%
  THEN Use Battery Mode

// Future: ML-based sunlight prediction, driving pattern analysis
